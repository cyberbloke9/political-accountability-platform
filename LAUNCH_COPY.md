### THE CREW (How It Works)

**1. The Promise Keepers**
Citizens submit political promises with exact quotes, dates, and credible sources. No editorializing. No opinions. Just cold, hard facts.

**2. The Verification Brigade**
Community members upload evidence‚Äîdocuments, images, news reports, official statements. Mark promises as fulfilled, broken, in progress, or stalled. Provide proof, not propaganda.

**3. The Voting Council**
The community votes on verification quality. Not on party lines. Not on political preference. **On evidence quality alone.**

**4. The Trust System**
Everyone starts at 100 reputation points. Quality contributions build your score. Gaming the system destroys it.

- **Untrusted (0.5x):** New sailors learning the ropes
- **Community (1.0x):** Regular crew members
- **Trusted (2.0x):** Veteran navigators
- **Admin (3.0x):** Ship captains

Your trust level determines your influence. Earn it through truth.

**5. The Anti-Gaming Armada**
Self-verification? **0.1x penalty.** Vote brigades? **Detected and crushed.** AI-generated content? **Immediate permanent ban.** Fake accounts? **IP banned forever.**

We're not here to play games. We're here to demand accountability.
